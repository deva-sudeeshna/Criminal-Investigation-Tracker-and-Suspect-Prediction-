Service One

To speed up the process of investigation and track status of ongoing case by predicting out the primary suspects on the basis of the records which consists of compendium of the people associated to the case, former criminal background proofs recovered from crime location, etc.

02

Service Two

To make the work easy by introducing the digitalized system for police department to check the status of the cases online and allow them to add up the new important information related to the case as when needed.

03

Service Three

Helps to keep track of the investigation status of the criminal cases with logs, to analyse the records in database and to predict the primary suspects. Our application allows to work collectively on cases and to update information reagrding the clues and suspects of cases.

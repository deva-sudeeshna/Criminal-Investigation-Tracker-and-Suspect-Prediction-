About Us

Whenever a case against the crime is filed the investigation always starts from the scratch right away from the evidences found at the crime location and the eye witnesses present at the crime location. To speed up the process of investigation and track status of ongoing case by predicting out the primary suspects on the basis of the records which consists of compendium of the people associated to the case, former criminal background proofs recovered from crime location, etc. This digitalized system makes the work easy for the police department to check the status of the case online and even allow them to add up the new important information related to the case as when needed and helps to analyse the primary suspects.
